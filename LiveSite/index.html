meet bob

Bob is a 21 year old college student who is a part of many student clubs on campus. As a leader, he has to organize multiple events for different focus groups. He often spends hours before an event manually creating playlists that might suit the mood of the activity. Although he knows a lot about music, he wishes he could spend less time on creating playlists and more time on organizing the events.

bob likes music with different themes

Bob selects themes about the event from an in-built checkbox. This checkbox includes tags such as “dance”, “formal”, “chill” and “high-energy” to help Bob determine the nature of the event and the people attending it. He doesn’t want his music taste to influence the music played at the event, so he selects broad tags. He also specifies the duration of the event (for example, he wants to play music for 2 hours). Once he’s done making his selection, he gets a list of relevant music that satisfy his requirements and is close to the given duration. This is generated in the form of a playlist. He can add and remove songs at any time. When he removes a song, he gets a suggestion for another similar song that matches his tags. He can choose to add this to his playlist if he wishes.
